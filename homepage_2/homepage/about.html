About Us

The story behind ARTICOM

What is ARTICOM?

ARTICOM is a platform built for artists and creatives to showcase their work, connect with other artists, and find opportunities that match their craft. Whether you're an illustrator, 3D modeler, animator, or sticker artist — ARTICOM is your space to grow, collaborate, and be discovered.

Our Mission

Our mission is to bridge the gap between artistic talent and real-world opportunity. We believe every creative deserves a platform that takes their work seriously — a place where art meets occupation, and passion becomes profession.
